WELCOME!



HEO (Help Elevate Each Other) is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering young people, particularly those from underprivileged backgrounds, through education and skill development in the field of technology and computer science. We believe that access to quality technological literacy and computer science education is crucial for promoting national development, creating jobs, and addressing the challenges of unemployment and poverty. Our goal is to provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment where young people can acquire the skills and knowledge they need to build successful careers in the tech industry and contribute positively to the nation.
